Put your static files here:

- resume.pdf   -> your resume, linked from data/site.ts (resumeUrl)
- avatar.jpg   -> your profile photo, if you want to swap the initials
  avatar in components/sections/Hero.tsx for a real photo
- favicon.ico  -> replace the default favicon

Anything in /public is served from the site root, e.g.
/public/resume.pdf becomes yoursite.com/resume.pdf
